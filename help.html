Help

This app has only been tested for Google Chrome. It may or may not work on other browsers.

How to build a tree:

Mark the opening of constituents with an opening square bracket followed immediately by the category of the constituent. Leave a space between the category and anything to follow. This app will build the tree as you type and will attempt to close any brackets that you may be missing.

Examples:

[NP Alice]
[NP [N Alice] and [N Bob]]
[S[NP[N Alice]][VP[V is][NP[N'[N a student][PP of physics]]]]]
