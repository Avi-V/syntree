Help

This app provides two ways to create trees:

The Simple Way

Mark the opening of constituents with an opening square bracket followed
immediately by the category of the constituent. Leave a space between
the category and anything to follow, except when you want to create a
simple unlabelled node.

Examples:

[NP Alice]
[NP [N Alice] [and] [N Bob]]
[S[NP[N Alice]][VP[V is][NP[N'[N'[D a][N student]][PP of physics]]]]]
<NP>Alice</NP>
